# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive.
- Giving and receiving feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insulting or derogatory comments.
- Public or private abuse.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards
and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces and in public spaces when an
individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior by opening a private maintainer contact issue
or reaching maintainers directly.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
